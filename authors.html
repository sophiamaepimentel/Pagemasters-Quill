Roshani Chokshi

Roshani Chokshi is an American novelist who is the author of her young adult novels The Star-touched Queen and A Crown of Wishes plus a collection of short stories called Star-touched Stories. The Star Touched Queen was a finalist for the 2017 Locus Award for Best First Novel and for 2017 Andre Norton Award. She recently came out last March of 2018 with a Rick Riordan presents middle grade book called Aru Shah and The End of Time. And in 2019, her new young adut novel The Gilded Wolves is set to come out.

Author Fun Fact: Roshani Chokshi is actually Asian despite the fact that she is considered an American novelist. Her father is Indian and her mother on the otherhand is a Filipina. And it was through her mixed heritage that she got the inspiration from in writing her books most especially Aru Shah and the End of Time.

Ransom Riggs

Ransom Riggs is the New York Times bestselling author of the Miss Peregrine’s Home for Peculiar Children series in which the first book was adapted into a film last 2016. He is also a filmmaker who worked on his short films for the Internet and blogging for Mental Floss which got him started on his writing career. After the release of his other books in the series: Hollow City and Library of Souls including a collection of short stories called Tales of the Peculiar, he took a short break from the author’s spotlight for two years and came back in October 2, 2018, releasing a continuation the Peculiar series which is called a Map of Days.

Author Fun Fact: Ransom Riggs is married to another beloved author in the publishing industry whose name is Tahereh Mafi. Tahereh Mafi wrote the bestselling Shatter Me series as well as her other two middle grade novels, Furthermore and Whichwood. She recently came out this 2018 with her latest installment continuation in the Shatter Me series, called Restore Me. As of last year (2017), the power couple’s first child (daughter) was born.

Renee Ahdieh

Renee Ahdieh is an American author of young adult fantasy, best known for her New York Times bestselling series The Wrath and the Dawn Duology. Due to the success of the aforementioned novel, she soon released the sequel The Rose and the Dagger in April 26, 2016, a year after The Wrath and the Dawn came out. In 2016, she also published a collection of three short stories that takes place in the same world as her first duology. Respectively they are: The Crown and the Arrow, The Moth and the Flame, and The Mirror and the Maze. Her second series was called The Flame in the Mist duology consisting of the Flame in the Mist and Smoke in the Sun (published in June 5 of 2018). Fans are excited after receiving the news of getting a new paranormal romance series from the author entitled: The Beautiful series set to come out October 8, 2019.

Author Fun Fact: Renee Ahdieh much like Roshani Chokshi is one of the few diverse authors that readers get to have today. She is half Korean and half Scottish. Her husband, Victor, on the otherhand, is Persian hence her last name. As a child of mixed race, she had been always drawn to novels that supported diversity and it led her to write her own stories expanding on different perspectives and wants to create characters that show varying levels of strength. Their pet dog is also named after a favourite Disney animated Chinese character in the movie Mulan, named Mushu.
