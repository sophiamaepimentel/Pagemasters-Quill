Hello and Welcome!

Pagemaster’s Quill is an established website featuring underrated literature books as well as underrated authors in today’s publishing industry. The aim of our website is for these underrated stories to be discovered even more and to expose the brilliant writers behind them. Alongside the underrated recommendations are updated news about the latest books, book to TV-movie adaptations and more! This webpage was founded by the DEMIGOD DIARIES group consisting of the following people: Khristine Rocel Osoteo, Sophia Mae Pimentel, Ma. Lyneber Arciaga and Antonio Jeremiah Turzar. Cassandra Clare at Bookcon 2018

As said before from the previous October book haul, the finale to The Dark Artifices by Cassandra Clare is set to come out for the month of December. The Dark Artifices is the third series in the Shadowhunter Chronicles that Cassie Clare has published alongside her other companion novels, The Bane Chronicles and The Shadowhunter Academy. The series takes place after the events of The Mortal Instruments following a young shadowhunter named Emma Carstairs and her parabatai Julian Blackthorn in modern day Los Angeles as she embarks on a mission to find out who killed her parents during the Dark War. Lady Midnight is a book worth rereading and so is Lord of Shadows. It is the best series thus far that truly reflected how much Cassie’s writing has evolved or changed, therefore has become better over the years. It is both excitement and a torture to wait for the third and last book to come out. Will everything truly change with the shadow world? Fingers crossed, it will. To know more details from the author, watch an interview made by Michael from team Epic Reads exclusively with the one and only author, Cassandra Clare.
